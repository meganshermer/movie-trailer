Captain America: The First Avenger

Slogan for this movie. Laugh/Cry/Scream -- out loud.

Steve Rogers, a rejected military soldier transforms into Captain America after taking a dose of a "Super-Soldier serum". But being Captain America comes at a price as he attempts to take down a war monger and a terrorist organization.

This Movie is one of my favorite Marvel movies because of the cinamatic beauty of the film. It is set in the historical time of WW2 and focuses on the concept of war and what a man will do to do the right thing by himself and his country. The plot is excuted perfectly and also sets up the oppurtunity for future movie
